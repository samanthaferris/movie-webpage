In this epic zom-com, the Wild West finds itself to have a new arch-nemesis(s); zombies! Due to an outbreak, cowboys are turned into these brain-eating zombies. With Claude Copper and co., they will fend off these zombies, and crack a few jokes along the way, as well as breaking the fifth wall.
